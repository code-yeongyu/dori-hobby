# Pokémon White — New Game → Striaton Trio Badge

## Starter Recommendation: Oshawott
Reasoning: Striaton's gym leader matches your starter's type-disadvantage — pick Oshawott → fight Cilan (Grass) → Pansage Lv.14. The Dreamyard NPC will gift you Pansear (Fire), which 4×-ones Pansage with Ember.

## Phase 1 — Nuvema Town
- Receive starter from Prof. Juniper's gift box (pick OSHAWOTT)
- Rival battle 1: Bianca (Lillipup Lv.6 + Snivy Lv.7) — Tackle/Tail Whip
- Rival battle 2: Cheren (Lillipup Lv.6 + Tepig Lv.7) — Cheren gives 3 Oran Berries
- Exit, talk to Mom, head north to Route 1

## Phase 2 — Route 1
- Juniper's catch tutorial (Minccino demo)
- Receive 5 Poké Balls
- Bianca starts "Catch 2" Pokémon Hunt (optional, ignore)
- NO wild trainers — straight north to Accumula

## Phase 3 — Accumula Town
- Meet Juniper at Pokémon Center (heal + buy Potions + Poké Balls)
- Team Plasma cutscene (Ghetsis speech — unskippable)
- Rival battle 3: N (Purrloin Lv.7) — must win to proceed
- Head west to Route 2

## Phase 4 — Route 2
- 3 trainers (all Lv.7): Jimmy/Patrat, Mali/Purrloin, Roland/Lillipup
- Items: 2 Potions, 1 Poké Ball
- Mom appears mid-route — gives Running Shoes (hold B to run)
- Rival battle 4: Bianca (Lillipup Lv.6 + her starter Lv.7) at route end
- Recommended team level entering Striaton: 8–10

## Phase 5 — Striaton City Pre-Gym (do BEFORE entering gym)

### 5a. Dreamyard (east of city)
- 2 trainers: Eri/Purrloin×2 Lv.8, Joey/Patrat+Patrat+Lillipup Lv.7
- After clearing trainers, talk to the girl — she gifts the type-counter monkey:
  - Oshawott starter → **Pansear** (Fire) — REQUIRED for Cilan
  - Tepig starter → Pansage (Grass) — REQUIRED for Cress
  - Snivy starter → Panpour (Water) — REQUIRED for Chili
- SAVE before next phase (X menu → Save)

### 5b. Trainer's School (west of Pokémon Center)
- Mandatory battle: Cheren (Purrloin Lv.9 + his starter Lv.9)
- Use your monkey here, it's super-effective on his starter
- Quiz: answer 2 questions to receive a Full Heal

### 5c. Gym Entry
- Clyde (gym guide) gives Fresh Water at door — accept
- SAVE before entering

## Phase 6 — Striaton Gym (Trio Badge)

### Layout
The gym has 3 type-coded curtain panels (water droplet / leaf / fire). Step on the panel matching your starter's WEAKNESS to advance. Choose:
- Oshawott pick → step on LEAF panel → fight waiter then Cilan
- Tepig pick → step on WATER panel → fight waiter then Cress
- Snivy pick → step on FIRE panel → fight waiter then Chili

### Boss (Oshawott path → Cilan)
- Cilan's Lillipup Lv.12 (Normal) — Oshawott's Water Gun OHKOs or 2HKOs
- Cilan's Pansage Lv.14 (Grass) — SWITCH to Pansear, use Ember (4× effective, 2HKO guaranteed)
- If low HP, use Fresh Water or Potions (X menu → Bag)

### Reward
- Trio Badge (Pokémon up to Lv.20 obey; enables Cut outside battle)
- TM83 Work Up (+Attack +Sp.Atk)

## Save Strategy (BW HAS NO AUTOSAVE)
- Save MANUALLY via X menu → Save before:
  - Every rival battle
  - Every gym leader battle
  - Entering Dreamyard
  - Entering the Gym
- One save slot per cart. Cannot overwrite without deleting (Up+Select+B at title).

## Type Matchup Cycle (memorize)
Your starter < Gym Leader's ace < Your Dreamyard monkey < Your starter
